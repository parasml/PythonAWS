#------------------------------------------------------------
# Data Lake Rollup/Rolldown of (parquet, JSON, Csv)
#-----------------------
Language Used: Python
Developer: Prashan Bawari

Objective:
To rollup/rolldown curated parquet files in s3 bucket.

Project Description:
1) Rollup/rolldown files at hour, day and month level
2) Hour buffer of 30 mins
3) Day buffer at 3:00 am of next day
4) Month rollup at 2nd saturday of every month.

Class Files:
1) main.py
    - Entry of 'Injestion' functionality
2) ECS_handler.py
    - Read SQS and get all the information e.g bucket, s3key etc
3) consolidateFiles.py
    - rollup or rolldown files of particular partition.
4) postgresDB.py
    - To connect AWS postgres DB and update new fields.
5) LogError.py   #Still need to work on this-----
    - To log errors.
6) dockerfile.txt
    - To create docker image.


AWS Services used:
1) S3  -->  (curated: sterling-datalake-curated-data-int-us-east-1)
2) ECR, ECS --> (ECR: datalake-compact-parquet-datalower) (datalake-inventory)
3) Postgres RDS --> (curated: curated_consolidated_metadata)


Deployment:
Deployed on AWS ECS (Fragate)
